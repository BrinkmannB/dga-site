Role and responsibility

The EU AI Act distinguishes between providers and deployers. Correctly identifying your role is essential to understanding which obligations apply.

2. Role of your organisation

Responsibility under the AI Act follows control and influence, not contracts alone. Public organisations are often deployers, but may still carry governance duties depending on how the AI system is configured and used.

Note: In the AI Act, responsibility follows control and influence over design and use. Public organisations may carry governance obligations even when using third-party systems.
