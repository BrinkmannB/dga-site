About Bas

Independent AI Governance Consultant for government, financial sector and society.

Responsible AI starts with good governance

I help organisations make conscious, transparent and responsible choices in the development and deployment of AI. With a background in governance, ethics and organisational behaviour, I specialise in:

  • AI governance frameworks
  • EU AI Act compliance
  • Risk assessments & transparency
  • Human-centric and ethical AI practices

My approach is independent, pragmatic and aimed at helping organisations build trust — both internally and with the outside world.
