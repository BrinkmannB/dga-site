The Core Question

Under what conditions can an organization remain free to act?

This question sits at the center of all DGA work.

As organizations become increasingly dependent on platforms, suppliers, AI systems and digital ecosystems, the conditions under which they can act independently begin to change.

Often gradually.

Often invisibly.

The Foundational Observation

Dependency rarely appears as a strategic decision.

Most dependencies emerge through decisions that improve convenience, efficiency, optimization, automation and integration.

Individually, these decisions make sense.

Collectively, they may reshape strategic autonomy.

Human beings depend on each other. Organizations depend on ecosystems. Societies depend on cooperation. Dependency is not an exception. It is the condition under which we live. The question is not whether dependency exists. The question is whether it is understood.
