Services

I help public institutions implement AI responsibly, transparently and in full compliance with the EU AI Act. Below is an overview of the core services I offer within ministries, municipalities and public-sector organisations.

AI Act Implementation

Practical guidance and full support in meeting AI Act obligations — including risk classification, technical documentation, conformity requirements and ongoing monitoring.

  • AI system classification (Annex III & GPAI)
  • Risk management & documentation
  • High-risk compliance readiness
  • Governance and internal controls

Algorithm Registers

I support governments in designing, improving and publishing transparency profiles in line with Dutch and EU requirements.

  • Transparency profiles
  • Public algorithm registers
  • Quality & risk assessments
  • Domain-specific guidance

AI Governance Frameworks

Setting up the organisation-wide structures needed for responsible AI — including roles, audits, monitoring and accountability processes.

  • Governance structures & roles
  • Audits and internal control
  • Ethical & legal alignment
  • Workshops and training

Interested in working together?

Whether you’re preparing for the AI Act or improving transparency around algorithms: I’m happy to support your organisation.
