EU AI Act Calculator

Determine your AI system’s risk classification and understand which obligations apply under the EU AI Act.

How the Calculator Works

The EU AI Act introduces risk-based categories for AI systems. This calculator helps you assess:

  • Whether your system is considered Minimal, Limited or High Risk
  • What compliance measures are required
  • Which documentation must be created
  • What transparency obligations apply
  • Which governance safeguards you must implement

Why This Matters

Public-sector organisations must ensure that AI systems impacting citizens’ rights meet strict requirements. The calculator offers a first indication of your obligations — helping you understand risks early and plan governance steps effectively.

Start the Risk Assessment

Answer a few quick questions about your system’s purpose, context, data and decision-making role. You will receive an indicative risk classification along with next steps.
