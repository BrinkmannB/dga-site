AI Governance Quick Scan

Five clear steps to assess risks, governance maturity and AI Act compliance.

How the Quick Scan Works

The Quick Scan provides a clear assessment within one week. It gives insight into risks, data usage, transparency requirements and obligations under the European AI Act.

Step 1 — Intake & Scoping

Short assessment of the AI system, its purpose, data flows and stakeholders.

Step 2 — Risks & Public Values

Analysis of risks related to harm, bias, explainability, transparency and societal impact.

Step 3 — AI Act Check

Initial determination of whether the system qualifies as high-risk and which obligations apply.

Step 4 — Governance & Maturity

Assessment of documentation, oversight, processes, risk controls and data quality.

Step 5 — Final Report

A concise report including findings, risks, improvement steps and compliance priorities.

Example Output

A clear, compact report including:

  • risk overview (technical, legal, organisational)
  • applicable AI Act obligations
  • governance and maturity scores
  • practical improvements that can be implemented immediately
  • recommended next steps toward compliance

Who Is It For?

Suitable for governments, financial institutions, NGOs and organisations that want:

  • clear insight into AI risks
  • quick understanding of AI Act obligations
  • responsible, transparent AI practices
  • to prepare for future audits or oversight
