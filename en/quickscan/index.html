Purpose and scope

The EU AI Act introduces differentiated obligations depending on the role, context and risk profile of AI systems. Many public organisations struggle to determine where they stand and what requires attention first.

This Quick Scan provides a structured first indication, aligned with EU AI Act logic and public-sector governance practice. It supports internal reflection and informed decision-making.

What this Quick Scan is — and is not

  • ✔ An indicative governance-focused assessment
  • ✔ Designed for governments and public institutions
  • ✖ Not a legal opinion
  • ✖ Not a technical audit
