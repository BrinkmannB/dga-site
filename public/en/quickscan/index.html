AI Governance Quick Scan

Concise, professional and ready for public sector use

1. Purpose

The AI Governance Quick Scan provides a compact, practice-oriented assessment of how AI and data are used within a selected project or organisation. It offers insight into governance gaps, risks, data quality, transparency and EU AI Act obligations.

2. Why now?

Public sector organisations increasingly deploy data-driven and AI-enabled systems. With the EU AI Act approaching, early governance alignment is essential to safeguard public values such as transparency, fairness, proportionality and human oversight. The Quick Scan immediately clarifies risks, governance gaps and necessary steps toward compliance.

3. Approach (1–2 weeks)

Phase 1 — Intake & scoping
Clarifying objectives, expected outcomes and reviewing available documentation.

Phase 2 — Governance, risk & data flow assessment
Mapping data sources, identifying risks (bias, transparency, explainability, minimisation), assessment against public value principles.

Phase 3 — Public values & ethics
Transparency requirements, human oversight, ethical and societal impact.

Phase 4 — AI Act impact assessment
Determining the risk category, overview of mandatory obligations, gap analysis.

Phase 5 — Final report & recommendations
Clear priorities, governance improvements, and an AI Act readiness matrix.

4. Deliverables

A professional assessment report, governance and risk overview, and practical recommendations for responsible and legally robust AI deployment.
