About Bas Brinkmann

AI Governance Consultant supporting governments and public-sector organisations in implementing responsible, transparent and legally aligned AI.

My Mission

I help organisations navigate the growing complexity of AI governance. My work focuses on enabling public institutions to use AI in a way that strengthens:

  • public trust
  • democratic legitimacy
  • explainability and transparency
  • risk-aware, accountable decision-making
  • alignment with the EU AI Act and fundamental rights

The goal: AI that serves society — responsibly, clearly and with proper safeguards.

My Background

I combine experience in governance, ethics, risk analysis and public-sector digital transformation. My work bridges legal, operational and ethical perspectives, making complex requirements practical and actionable.

This includes reviewing AI systems, building governance frameworks, conducting explainability analysis and preparing organisations for EU AI Act obligations.

How I Work

  • Independent, structured and transparent
  • Focused on feasibility, not theory
  • Clear documentation and decision-making structures
  • Respect for public values and institutional responsibility
