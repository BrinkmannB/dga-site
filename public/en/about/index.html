About Bas Brinkmann

AI Governance Consultant — working at the intersection of technology, regulation and public values.

Who I am

I help organisations design, evaluate and implement AI systems that are responsible, explainable and aligned with societal expectations.

My work is grounded in the belief that AI should strengthen public trust, support professionals in their work, and contribute to better decisions — not undermine them.

My approach

1. Clear & pragmatic

No jargon. I translate complex AI risks and regulatory language into practical insights decision-makers and teams can actually use.

2. Independent & thorough

Every assessment starts with a simple principle: Is this responsible, explainable and proportionate?

3. Grounded in real-world implementation

Governance frameworks matter — but only when they work in practice. I focus on solutions that fit the operational reality of your organisation.

What drives me

  • Trust — AI must be reliable and understandable.
  • Transparency — organisations should know how systems work and why they make certain decisions.
  • Societal impact — technology should contribute to public good, not undermine it.

I’m happy to discuss how AI governance can strengthen your organisation.
