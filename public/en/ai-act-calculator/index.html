AI Act Calculator

Determine the risk category of your AI system

What the Calculator Does

This calculator helps you classify your AI system under the EU AI Act. The assessment is based on intended purpose, impact, autonomy, and potential risks.

Risk Categories

  • Minimal Risk – general-purpose, low-impact tools
  • Limited Risk – systems requiring transparency obligations
  • High Risk – systems impacting safety, fundamental rights or public services
  • Prohibited Practices – manipulative, exploitative or harmful systems
