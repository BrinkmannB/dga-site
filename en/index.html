Independent, public-interest driven AI governance

I support governments and public institutions in navigating the practical and legal implications of AI use. My work focuses on translating regulatory requirements — including the EU AI Act — into workable governance structures, transparency mechanisms and oversight arrangements.

The emphasis is not on technology itself, but on responsibility: who decides, who oversees, and how public values are safeguarded when AI systems are deployed.
