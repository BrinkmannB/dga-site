AI Act Impact Statement

A clear, legal and governance-based assessment of your AI system under the EU AI Act.

Why an AI Act Statement?

Organizations developing, purchasing or deploying AI systems must demonstrate compliance with the EU AI Act. An AI Act Statement outlines:

  • The nature and context of the AI system
  • The risk category under the AI Act
  • Governance structure and datastreams
  • Organizational and technical safeguards
  • Transparency, explainability and fairness requirements
  • Alignment with public values

What does it deliver?

  • A complete document for audits, steering groups and regulators
  • Clear legal positioning of the AI system
  • Insight into required follow-up actions
  • Compliance-ready foundation for AI Act implementation
