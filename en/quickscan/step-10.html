What type of AI system is being used?

Select the option that best reflects the AI system as it is actually used in practice. This step provides a final, indicative governance signal and does not replace a formal legal qualification.

Foundation model / generative AI system (GPAI)
General-purpose AI that can be deployed or adapted across multiple use cases and may be subject to additional transparency and governance obligations under the AI Act.
Application-specific AI system
AI used in a specific context with potential direct impact on decisions, rights or access to services, possibly subject to high-risk obligations under the AI Act.
Simple or low-risk AI system
Supportive or informational use of AI with limited impact and no clear indication of increased AI Act obligations.

This selection affects only the indicative governance signal and does not constitute a legal or binding classification.
