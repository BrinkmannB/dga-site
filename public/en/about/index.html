About Bas

Independent AI governance consultant — focused on risk, transparency and public values.

Who I Am

I’m Bas Brinkmann, an AI governance consultant specialising in risk assessment, transparency and compliance with the European AI Act. My work centres on responsible, explainable and legally grounded use of AI.

I support governments, financial institutions and civil society organisations in building trustworthy and transparent AI systems.

My Approach

Independent, pragmatic and clear. I avoid unnecessary complexity and focus on risks, public values and proportionate governance.

  • clear guidance without jargon
  • risk-focused, value-based approach
  • AI Act expertise combined with governance experience
  • practical and actionable recommendations

Who I Work With

Governments, financial institutions, NGOs and organisations that want to use AI responsibly, transparently and in line with legal and societal expectations.
