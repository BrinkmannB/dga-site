About Bas Brinkmann

I am an AI Governance Consultant with a focus on government, public institutions and civil society organisations. I support responsible, transparent and legally robust AI development and deployment, fully aligned with the EU AI Act.

My work is centred around clarity: understanding risks, identifying obligations, and translating the AI Act into concrete governance structures and practices.

I combine analytical strength with a practical, human-centred approach. For me, AI governance is not just about compliance — it’s about trust. Citizens must be able to rely on technology that is fair, explainable and safe.

I contribute to strategy, policy, implementation, risk classification and governance frameworks — from first scan to full AI Act readiness.
