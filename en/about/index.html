About Bas Brinkmann

Independent advisor on AI governance, public values and the EU AI Act.

I work with governments, public institutions and non-profit organisations to help them apply AI in a responsible and human-centric way. My focus is on the intersection of governance, legal compliance, organisational design and public values.

In my work, I combine AI governance frameworks with practical, accessible guidance for decision-makers. Whether it is risk classification under the EU AI Act, transparency obligations or human-AI collaboration, my approach is rooted in clarity, context and accountability.

I regularly write and speak about the broader societal impact of AI, including democratic accountability, public-value alignment and the organisational dynamics of AI adoption.

My mission: helping organisations design AI in a way that strengthens human judgement, protects fundamental rights and supports the public interest.
