Why this conversation?

Most organizations believe they are managing technology. In reality, they may be managing the consequences of accumulated dependency. Cloud platforms. AI systems. External suppliers. Digital ecosystems. Over time these dependencies shape decision-making, constrain strategic options and influence organizational autonomy.

Questions we explore

Dependency

Where are critical dependencies emerging?

Control

Which assumptions about control may no longer be valid?

Autonomy

Which capabilities remain genuinely under organizational control?

Governance

How should governance evolve as dependencies accumulate?

Who is it for?

Board members. Executives. Public leaders. Policy makers. Anyone responsible for strategic decisions in increasingly complex digital ecosystems.
