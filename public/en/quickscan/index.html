AI Governance Quick Scan

Immediate insight into AI Act compliance, risk management and governance maturity.

Why This Quick Scan?

AI systems impact people, rights and public trust. The EU AI Act requires organisations to classify systems, manage risks, demonstrate transparency and ensure human oversight.

This Quick Scan gives you a **first impression** of where your organisation stands — in less than a minute.

What You’ll Learn

  • Your AI governance maturity level
  • Potential AI Act compliance gaps
  • Key risks in transparency, fairness and oversight
  • Whether documentation and processes are sufficient
  • Where to focus your next steps
