About Bas Brinkmann

Independent AI Governance Consultant for public sector organisations.

Who I Am

I work as an independent AI Governance Consultant, specialising in AI Act implementation, accountability frameworks, risk management and the alignment of AI systems with public values.

My work focuses on bridging the gap between legal obligations, technical realities and ethical considerations. I help organisations make AI understandable, transparent and responsibly governed — without losing sight of practical feasibility.

My Focus Areas

  • AI Act classification and compliance
  • Risk, impact and fundamental rights assessments
  • Governance structures & oversight design
  • Algorithmic transparency and explainability
  • Public value protection and trustworthy AI practices

How I Work

I collaborate with public sector organisations, governmental bodies, regulators and institutions navigating emerging European AI regulations. My approach is independent, practical and grounded in responsibility toward citizens, fairness and societal impact.
