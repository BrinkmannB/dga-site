AI Act • Governance QuickScan

Indicative governance signal

This outcome provides a first indication of how responsibility, oversight and decision-making around AI are structured in practice.

What this outcome does indicate

This QuickScan highlights how AI governance currently functions within your organisation based on your responses. It points out where governance appears deliberate and where potential vulnerabilities or ambiguities may exist.

The outcome is intended as a professional orientation point — a way to surface governance questions, not to resolve them.

What this outcome does not indicate

  • ✕ No legal judgment or binding qualification
  • ✕ No formal AI Act risk classification
  • ✕ No compliance audit or assessment
  • ✕ No substitute for legal or technical analysis

Governance reflection

If this QuickScan raises questions, that is not a weakness but the core purpose of the instrument. AI governance requires explicit choices, clear accountability and insight into actual practice — not merely policies on paper.

The relevant follow-up question is therefore not: “Is this right or wrong?”, but: “Is this governable, explainable and sustainable?”
