EU AI Act Categories Covered

Minimal-Risk AI

Free use under the EU AI Act. Recommended to maintain basic documentation.

Limited-Risk AI

Transparency obligations may apply depending on the use-case.

High-Risk AI

Full compliance requirements including risk management, documentation, logging and human oversight.

Prohibited AI

Not allowed to be placed on the EU market or used by public entities.
