AI Governance Quick Scan

Five clear steps to assess risks, transparency and AI Act alignment.

Step 1 — Risk Analysis

Assessment of potential harms, bias, data quality and rights impacts.

Step 2 — Transparency & Explainability

Documentation review, model behaviour, audit trails and explainability.

Step 3 — Governance & Accountability

Evaluation of roles, responsibilities, oversight and organisational controls.

Step 4 — AI Act Classification

Determining whether a system is high-risk, limited-risk or prohibited.

Step 5 — Recommendations

Practical next steps: policy updates, safeguards and transparency measures.
