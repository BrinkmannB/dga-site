Purpose of this QuickScan

The EU AI Act introduces different obligations depending on the context, purpose and risk profile of AI systems. Organisations often struggle to determine where they stand and which questions require attention first.

This QuickScan offers a structured starting point. It follows the logic of the AI Act itself — from prohibited practices and high-risk classification to governance and oversight.

What the QuickScan does — and does not do

  • Provides an indicative overview of relevant AI Act themes
  • Helps structure internal reflection and discussion
  • Does not provide legal advice or formal classification
  • Does not replace a full assessment or compliance trajectory

What to expect

  • Approximately 3 minutes
  • No technical expertise required
  • Aligned with the structure of the EU AI Act
  • No account, no data retention

The QuickScan is currently available in Dutch. Its structure and logic are equally applicable in an international public-sector context.
