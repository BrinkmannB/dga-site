What this QuickScan does

The AI Act introduces different obligations depending on the purpose, context and risk profile of AI systems. This QuickScan helps you reflect on those aspects in a structured way.

The scan consists of a limited number of steps that follow the logic of the AI Act itself — from prohibited practices and high-risk classification to governance and oversight.

What to expect

  • Approximately 3 minutes
  • No technical expertise required
  • Aligned with the structure of the EU AI Act
  • Indicative outcome, not legal advice

The QuickScan is currently available in Dutch. The structure and outcomes are equally applicable in an international public-sector context.
