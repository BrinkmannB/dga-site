Step 10 of 10 — Type of AI system

Select the option that best reflects the AI system as it is actually used in practice. This provides a final, indicative governance signal.

Foundation model / generative AI system (GPAI)

General-purpose AI that can be deployed or adapted across multiple use cases, subject to additional transparency and governance obligations.

Application-specific AI system

AI with direct impact on decisions, rights or access to services, potentially subject to high-risk obligations under the AI Act.

Simple or low-risk AI system

Supportive or informational use with limited impact and no clear indication of increased obligations.

This selection is indicative and does not constitute a legal qualification.
