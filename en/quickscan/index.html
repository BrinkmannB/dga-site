AI Governance Quick Scan

Understand your AI system’s risk level, compliance obligations and governance gaps under the EU AI Act.

What the Quick Scan Provides

The Quick Scan gives you a structured overview of the risks, responsibilities and documentation requirements connected to your AI system. It is designed specifically for governmental and public-sector organisations working with high-impact or AI-assisted decision-making processes.

  • Risk classification under the EU AI Act
  • Required documentation (Technical File, logs, transparency requirements)
  • Governance gaps and areas needing improvement
  • Explainability and ethical considerations
  • Recommendations for responsible and compliant use

When to Request a Quick Scan

You should consider a Quick Scan if your organisation is:

  • Developing or deploying an AI-based decision support tool
  • Using algorithms that affect citizens’ rights or access to services
  • Unsure whether your system qualifies as high-risk under the EU AI Act
  • Preparing for internal audits, compliance reviews or public accountability reporting

Next Steps

After the Quick Scan, you will receive a concise report with recommended actions and a proposed governance approach. This helps you prioritise steps toward legal compliance and ethical implementation.
