About Bas Brinkmann

AI governance consultant working at the intersection of public values, regulation and responsible technology.

Who I am

I help governments, public institutions and NGOs design responsible AI practices — grounded in the EU AI Act, democratic values and practical, risk-based governance.

With a background in ethics, organisational governance and applied AI policy, I support organisations in strengthening transparency, fairness, oversight structures and risk-controls.

What I do

  • EU AI Act compliance advice (risk classification, documentation, governance)
  • AI governance maturity scans & implementation support
  • Workshops and training for boards, ethics committees and policy teams
  • Responsible AI frameworks tailored to public institutions

Mission

My work is built on one goal: enabling organisations to use AI responsibly, transparently and in service of society.
