Quick Scan — Step 5 of 10

Human oversight and accountability

The AI Act requires that AI systems are subject to meaningful human oversight, with clear accountability for decisions and outcomes.

5. Oversight and responsibility

This step examines whether people — not systems — ultimately remain responsible for decisions, and whether oversight is organised in a clear and effective way.

Why this matters: Human oversight is not a formality. It must be effective in practice, especially where AI affects rights, access to services or legal position.
