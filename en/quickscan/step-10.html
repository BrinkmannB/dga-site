What type of AI system is being used?

Select the option that best reflects the AI system as it is actually used in practice. The classification below provides a first indication of potential obligations under the EU AI Act and does not replace a formal legal qualification.

Foundation model / generative AI system (GPAI)
A general-purpose AI model that can be deployed or adapted for a wide range of use cases, subject to additional transparency and governance obligations under the AI Act
Application-specific AI system
AI with a direct impact on decisions, rights or access to services, potentially subject to high-risk obligations under the AI Act
Simple or low-risk AI system
Limited impact, supportive or informational use, with no clear indication of increased AI Act obligations
