About Bas

Independent AI governance consultant for governments, focused on AI Act implementation, transparency obligations and responsible AI deployment.

Who I am

I'm Bas Brinkmann, an AI Governance Consultant specialised in supporting public-sector institutions. I help organisations apply AI responsibly, transparently and in full legal compliance. With a blend of policy insight, technical understanding and analytical clarity, I translate complex regulations into practical, actionable solutions.

What I help organisations with

  • AI Act implementation and preparation
  • Risk assessments & technical documentation
  • Algorithmic transparency and public registers
  • AI governance frameworks and internal controls
  • Accountability and transparency processes
  • Policy advice on AI and automated decision-making

My approach

I work pragmatically, transparently and independently. Not lengthy reports without impact, but concrete steps that improve decision-making, compliance and public accountability. Always grounded in feasibility, legal requirements and public values.

Mission

My mission is to help public organisations deploy AI in a way that respects and protects citizens: transparent, fair, explainable and safe. Technology should strengthen democratic values, not undermine them.
