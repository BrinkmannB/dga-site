AI Act Statement

This statement explains how Dutch Governance Advisory approaches transparency, responsibility and compliance with the EU AI Act — both in advisory work and in the use of AI tools.

Purpose of this statement

The EU AI Act introduces obligations for developing, procuring and deploying AI systems. This statement clarifies how I operate in alignment with the Act’s core principles of transparency, explainability and human accountability.

No high-risk AI

I do not develop, deploy or use any AI system classified as high-risk under the EU AI Act. My own activities fall under the category “minimal risk”.
