What type of AI system is being used?

Select the option that best reflects the AI system as it is actually used in practice.

This final step provides an indicative governance signal regarding potential obligations under the EU AI Act. It does not constitute a legal qualification.

Foundation model / generative AI system (GPAI)
General-purpose AI that can be deployed or adapted across multiple use cases. Such systems may be subject to additional transparency and governance obligations under the AI Act.
Application-specific AI system
AI used within a specific context that may directly affect decisions, rights or access to services, and may therefore fall within high-risk AI Act obligations.
Simple or low-risk AI system
Supportive or informational use of AI with limited impact, and no clear indication of increased AI Act obligations.
