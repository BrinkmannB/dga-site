Services

Independent, practical and legally robust AI governance advisory for public bodies, NGOs and the financial sector.

AI Governance Assessment

A rapid maturity scan of your organisation’s AI landscape. I assess documentation, governance structures, risks, impact and your current compliance position.

  • Maturity scan (policy, roles, processes)
  • Risk evaluation of existing AI systems
  • AI Act alignment and compliance potential
  • Clear recommendations for governance improvements

AI Act Compliance Scan

Clear guidance on applicable obligations, risk classes and required documentation based on the EU AI Act.

  • Determination of risk class (prohibited, high-risk, limited, minimal)
  • Documentation and controls required by law
  • Gap analysis against the AI Act
  • Priority actions for the first 90 days

Algorithm Audit Light

  • Fairness & bias risk review
  • Model transparency & explainability
  • Data quality assessment
  • Audit-ready documentation

Risk & Impact Analysis (RIA)

Insight into legal, societal and ethical effects of AI systems.

  • Ethics & public values assessment
  • Legal risks and proportionality
  • Mitigation and governance options
