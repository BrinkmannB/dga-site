Risk management, monitoring and incidents

The AI Act requires that risks are not only identified, but also actively managed throughout the lifecycle of an AI system.

7. Ongoing risk management

This step looks at whether risks related to the AI system are monitored over time, and whether procedures exist to respond to incidents, errors or unexpected outcomes.
